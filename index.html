Why Plastic Pollution Matters

Plastic has transformed modern life — but at a devastating cost. From the deepest ocean trenches to the highest mountains, plastic pollution has reached every corner of our planet. Microplastics have been found in human blood, breast milk, and even unborn babies.

The good news? We have the knowledge, alternatives, and the power to change course. This website is your guide to understanding the problem and becoming part of the solution.
