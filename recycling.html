Recycling Guide

Learn the 3Rs (Reduce, Reuse, Recycle) and practical steps to recycle at home.

Reduce

Avoid single-use plastics and choose reusable products.

Reuse

Repurpose containers and donate usable items instead of discarding them.

Recycle

Sort and clean recyclables before placing them in proper bins. Check the recycling codes (1–7).

How to Recycle at Home

  • Separate recyclables from general waste.
  • Rinse food containers before recycling.
  • Check recycling numbers (1–7) and sort accordingly.
  • Locate nearby recycling centers here.
