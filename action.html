10 Ways to Reduce Your Plastic Footprint

Start with one or two changes and build from there. Every action counts.

#1 Shopping

Carry a reusable bag everywhere you go — keep one in your car, bag, or pocket.

#2 Beverages

Switch to a reusable water bottle and coffee cup. Refuse single-use cups at cafes.

#3 Food

Carry reusable cutlery and a straw when eating out or ordering takeaway.

#4 Packaging

Choose products with minimal or plastic-free packaging. Buy in bulk when possible.

#5 Personal Care

Switch to shampoo bars, conditioner bars, and solid soap to eliminate bathroom plastic.

#6 Groceries

Avoid plastic-wrapped produce. Shop at farmers markets or zero-waste stores.

#7 Recycling

Learn your local recycling rules and recycle correctly — contamination ruins batches.

#8 Community

Participate in local beach, park, or community clean-up events.

#9 Advocacy

Talk to friends, family, and colleagues about plastic reduction. Share what you learn.

#10 Consumer Power

Support businesses and brands that are committed to reducing plastic packaging.
